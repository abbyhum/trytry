      Cookies'n Cream

        Ingredients
      • 1 cup of butter (2 sticks)
      • 1 1/2 cups brown sugar
      • 1 cup of white sugar
      • 4 eggs
      • 5 cups of flour
      • 2 tsp baking soda
      • 2 tsp baking powder
      • 1 tsp salt
      • 4 cups of white chocolate chips
      • 4 cups of crushed (regular, not double stuffed) Oreos


        Prep
      • Place two sleeves of Oreos into a gallon size ziptop bag and crush with a rolling pin until the cookies are in large crumbs. Set aside.
      • Crack 4 eggs into a small bowl. Set aside.
      • In a different small bowl add 1 cup of the flour and the baking soda, baking powder and salt and combine with a whisk. Set aside.
      • Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper. Set aside.
      • Preheat oven to 400 degrees F


        Mix
      • Place softened butter and sugars in a mixing bowl and use a hand mixer until thoroughly combined.
      • Add eggs and mix again.
      • Add 4 cups of flour to the mixer, mix.
      • Add the reserved cup of flour with the baking soda/powder and salt into the mixer and mix until combined.
      • Finally, add the chocolate chips and crushed oreos and mix just until blended.


        Bake
      • Using a size cookie scoop, scoop rounded balls of dough onto the cookie sheet. Try to only put 9 cookie balls on each tray.
      • Bake in the oven for 8-10 minutes until the edges and tops of the cookies are lightly browned.
      • Let cool a few minutes on the cookie sheet and then move each cookie carefully to a cooling rack until set.
      • Repeat until all dough is cooked.
